    About Us

    Hero’s Shanzu Primary and Junior High School is a leading educational institution in Mombasa, Kenya, providing quality education and nurturing young minds to become future leaders.

    Welcome to Hero's AIC Shanzu primary and junior high school

    At Hero’s AIC Shanzu, we are committed to providing world-class education to our students, empowering them with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed in every aspect of their academic and personal lives.

    • Expert faculty

      • Our faculty comprises a team of expert educators, each with years of experience and a passion for teaching. They work tirelessly to ensure that every student receives the guidance and support they need to excel.

    • Comprehensive support

      • We provide comprehensive support to our students, including access to counseling, academic support, and extracurricular activities, ensuring that every student has the opportunity to thrive.

    Language Program

    Our Language Program offers students the opportunity to learn foreign languages, including English, French, and Spanish. With a focus on communication skills and cultural awareness, this program aims to equip students with the skills necessary to succeed in a globalized world.

    Our Enriching Classes

    At Hero’s A.I.C Shanzu Primary & Junior High School, we offer various classes to help our students develop their skills and interests. Our classes are designed to enhance creativity, critical thinking, and teamwork, preparing our students for a successful future.

    Education

    At Hero’s A.I.C Shanzu Primary & Junior High School, we take education seriously. Our teachers are dedicated to providing quality education that prepares our students for a successful future. We offer a well-rounded curriculum that includes math, science, language arts, and social studies, aiming to provide our students with the knowledge and skills they need to thrive.

    Arts and Crafts

    Our Arts and Crafts class provides a creative outlet for our students, allowing them to express themselves through various art forms. Our program includes painting, drawing, sculpture, and more, encouraging our students to explore their creativity and develop their artistic skills.

    Science Club

    Join our Science Club and explore the wonders of science with hands-on experiments and projects. Our club offers a fun and educational environment where students can learn about different fields of science and develop their scientific skills.

    Sports Club

    Join our Sports Club and stay active while developing your physical and social skills. Our program includes various sports, such as basketball, football, volleyball, and more, providing our students with a fun and engaging way to stay fit and healthy.

    Music Class

    Unleash your musical talents in our Music Class, where students can learn to play different instruments, read sheet music, and perform in front of an audience. Our program offers a comprehensive approach to music education, catering to learners of all levels, from beginners to advanced players.

    Drama Class

    Discover your passion for acting and storytelling in our Drama Class. Our program offers a comprehensive approach to theater education, including acting techniques, script analysis, and stage production, giving our students a well-rounded experience of the world of drama.

    Pre Primary School

    Our Pre Primary program, spanning PP1 to PP3, is designed to foster a love for learning in young children through engaging and age-appropriate activities.

    • We offer a stimulating environment where children can explore and discover through play-based learning.
    • Our curriculum focuses on developing essential skills such as literacy, numeracy, and social interaction.
    • With experienced and caring educators, we ensure that each child receives personalized attention to support their unique growth and development.

    Primary School

    Our Primary School program, encompassing Grade 1 to Grade 7, aims to provide a strong educational foundation through a comprehensive and dynamic curriculum.

    • We emphasize critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills to prepare students for future academic challenges.
    • Our curriculum covers a wide range of subjects, including language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies, ensuring a well-rounded education.
    • With dedicated and passionate teachers, we foster a supportive and nurturing learning environment where every child can thrive and achieve their full potential.

    Junior Secondary School

    Our Junior Secondary School program, covering grades 8 to 10, is designed to build on the foundational knowledge gained in primary school and to prepare students for senior secondary education.

    • We offer a rigorous and engaging curriculum that challenges students academically while supporting their individual interests and talents.
    • Our program includes a diverse range of subjects such as advanced mathematics, sciences, humanities, and technology to ensure a well-rounded education.
    • With experienced educators and a focus on personal development, we aim to cultivate confident, responsible, and independent learners ready for the next stage of their academic journey.
